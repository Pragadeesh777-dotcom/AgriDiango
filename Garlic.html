Open Pollinated Variety

Name of Variety: Red Skin Local Sweet Potato (Open Pollinated Variety)

Type: Open Pollinated

Where it is grown: Tamil Nadu (Erode, Salem, Namakkal, Dharmapuri, Villupuram, Cuddalore districts)

About: Plant is a trailing vine with heart-shaped leaves. Tuber is medium to large sized, reddish skin with yellowish flesh inside. Rich in carbohydrates, vitamin A, and dietary fiber.

Ooty 1 Garlic (Hill Garlic)

Name of Variety: Ooty 1 Garlic (also called Ooty Garlic / Hill Garlic)

Type: Traditional / Local (GI-tagged variety from Tamil Nadu)

Where it is grown: Nilgiris district (Ooty hills, Tamil Nadu)

About: Ooty Garlic is a small-bulbed garlic variety grown in the cool climate of the Nilgiris. It is well known for its unique pungent aroma and medicinal value.

Kodaikanal Garlic

Name of Variety: Kodaikanal Garlic (also called Hill Garlic of Kodaikanal)

Type: Traditional / Local

Where it is grown: Upper Palani Hills region around Kodaikanal (Dindigul district, Tamil Nadu)

About: Kodaikanal Garlic is a unique hill-grown garlic variety cultivated in the cool and misty climate of Kodaikanal. Prized for its intense pungency, medicinal qualities, and small, compact bulbs.
